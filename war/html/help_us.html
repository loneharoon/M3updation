Contributing to M3

Do not hesitate to share ideas, bugs, suggestions or feedback to

Are you an ontology-based domain expert?

Do not hesitate to share your domain ontologies, datasets and rules.
We are interested in various domains relevant for IoT such as healthcare, domotic, agriculture, transportation, etc.
We will reference them in LOV4IoT.

Are you an IoT developer?

Do not hesitate to ask us for new M3 templates, improve M3 documentations or fix bugs.

Are you a semantic web expert?

We have in mind to upgrade existing semantic web tools to enhance interoperability and reuse domain knowledge.
Tools: ontology or rule editor (e.g., Protege), ontology matching, converting rule format.

Are you a security expert?

Share your domain knowledge in a security domain such as Bluetooth, NFC, mobile phones.
We will upgrade our STAC knowledge base according to your state of the art.

Are you involved in standardizations?

Spread our M3 approach in standardizations: the M3 nomenclature and ontology, M3 domain knowledge, S-LOR, STAC, etc.

Do you believe in the M3 framework?

We are looking for fundings to upgrade M3.
We have so many ideas in mind.
Any ideas for fundings will be more than welcome.

Do you design awesome tools that we could interconnect with M3?

Such as Sensor Plug & Play (sensor discovery), real sensor, real sensor datasets, IoT real scenarios, service discovery, etc.
